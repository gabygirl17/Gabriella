About Our Website

This website was made to firstly create awareness about how our marine life is being destroyed and secondly so that you can learn more about how you can help to save our marine life . This website also allows you to sign up to help rescue marine animals as well as plant life. The website also informs you about the different effects of pollution on marine flora and fauna.

6 Things You Can Do To Save The Ocean Flora & Fauna

  • Reduce energy consumption
  • Make sustainable seafood choices
  • Use fewer plastic bags or items
  • Help take care of the beach
  • Be an ocean friendly pet owner
  • Get educated on oceans and marine life
