Reduce Energy Consumption

Reduce effects of climate change on the ocean by leaving the car at home when you can , and being conscious of your enrgy use at work.

Make sustainable seafood choices

When shopping or dining out , help reduce the demand for overexploited species by choosing seafood that is both healthy and sustainable.

Use Fewer Plastic Products

Carry a reusable water bottle, store food in a nondisposable container, use recycleable amd eco-friendly items as much as possible.

Help Take Care Of The Beach

Whether you enjoy diving,surfing,or relaxing,on the beach, always clean up after yourself. Explore and appreciate the ocean without interfering with wildlife or removing roacks and coral

Be An Ocean-Friendly Pet Owner

Avoid stocking your aquarium with wild-caught saltwater fish , and never release any aquarium fish into the ocean or any body or water that can introduce non-native species , harmful to existing ecosystems

Get Educated On Oceans And Marine Life

All life on Earth is connected to the ocean and its inhabitants. The more you learn about the issues facing this vital system , the more you'll want to help ensure its health - then share that knowledge to educate and inspire others
